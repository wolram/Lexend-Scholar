Frequently Asked Questions

Can we switch plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

What does institutional onboarding look like?

Pro and Enterprise plans include a guided 4-week onboarding process, including data migration from your previous SIS and staff training sessions.

Are there discounts for non-profits?

Yes! We offer a 20% discount for registered non-profit educational institutions and community learning centers. Contact support for details.

Do you charge for student storage?

No. Lexend Scholar provides unlimited storage for student assignments, portfolios, and academic records across all tiers.
